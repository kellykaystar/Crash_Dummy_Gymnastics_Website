How bad will this hurt?

Step 1
Find an open space near a wall where you probably won’t crash into furniture, or innocent bystanders.
Step 2
Stand tall like you actually know what you’re doing. Feet together, confidence high.
Step 3
Kick one leg up behind you like you’re trying to launch yourself and simultaneously, push off with your other leg.
