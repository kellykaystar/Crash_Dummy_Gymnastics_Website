Pain-O-Meter

How bad will this hurt?

Step 1
Find any hoverboard or two-wheeled motorized death trap and place it in an open area,
(preferably away from things you don’t want to crash into; walls, pets, or your dignity)
Step 2
Turn on the hoverboard. You should hear a beep that basically translates to “bad idea
detected.” This means it’s working perfectly.
Step 3
Stand directly one foot behind the hoverboard. Take a deep breath and maybe
say goodbye to your front teeth.
Step 4
Bend down and place each hand on either side of the hoverboard.
Try not to think about the fact that this thing was designed for feet, not hands..
Step 5
Stick your good foot out behind you like you’re about to impress an Olympic judge.
Step 6
Launch your back foot into the air as hard as humanly possible and hope for the best.
