Back Tuck
(No handed back flip)

Pain-O-Meter

How bad will this hurt?

Step 1
Flat surfaces are your friend—for now. Find a open flat surface
Step 2
Stand tall with your feet together, Both of them!
Step 3
Jump straight up and back, tucking your knees in like your life depends on it—because, well, it kind of does.
Step 4
Flip gracefully. Or chaotically. Either way, just commit and hope your head doesn’t meet the floor in a tragic way.
Step 5
If all goes well, land on both feet like you
totally meant to do that. If not… improvise.
Step 6
Strike a victory pose because you survived. Maybe throw in a dramatic hair flip.
Maybe rethink your life choices. Either works.
