Handstand
(No handed)

Pain-O-Meter

How bad will this hurt?

Step 1
Find an open space near a wall where you probablywon’t crash into
furniture, or innocent bystanders.
Step 2
Stand tall like you actually know what you’re doing. Feet together,
confidence high.
Step 3
Kick one leg up behind you like you’re trying to launch yourself and
simultaneously, push off with your other leg.
Step 4
Okay, now lift your hands. You don’t need them. Just let them vibe somewhere else while your body figures things out mid-air.
Step 5
If all goes well, your feet should find the ground again.
Step 6
Strike a victory pose. Chest up, chin high—like you just stuck the greatest trick of all time.
